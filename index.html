Aywas SCC Database!

Oh hi! Welcome to Aywas' Unofficial SCC Database: A reference for Scav Silvers and user-owned creations alike. This database keeps track of the species that are open to the public, whether PSD uses are purchased or available for free, as well as private/friend-only species. Unfortunately we will not be displaying Unique SCC lines here.

Want to track down an old favourite? Looking for some new lines to add to your lair? Or maybe you just want to browse through the artwork? Here you'll find images, links to species' and sale threads, Silver Potion status, and more!

If you know of any species that are not listed, fill out the form here and it will be added in the next round of updates.
